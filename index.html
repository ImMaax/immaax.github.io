Welcome

Welcome to my homepage! This site serves as a permanent and ever-changing exhibition space for my personal photography. See below for the albums which I have sorted my photos into.

As most of my work is in the analog domain, almost all the images on this website are scans of darkroom prints from my archive. Many of my prints were created in the lith process, a specialty technique among darkroom printers. I utilize equipment ranging from small 35mm SLRs and obscure toy cameras all the way to a big and heavy 4x5" view camera.

If you have any inquiries to make, do not hesitate to contact me. Also, if you would like to see more of my photos, especially more recent ones, feel free to visit my Flickr page:
