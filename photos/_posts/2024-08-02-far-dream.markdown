When I took this photo, it had been raining for a few days. The entire area was
flooded and became a moor, although the water pictured here is a normal lake. It
was gloomy, dark and hazy. This shot has a strong serenity and a feeling of a
far dream or old, faint memory to it, I think. It's almost a bit haunting or
eerie, even. The toning and the long exposure blur in the tree branches and
water certainly add to that.
