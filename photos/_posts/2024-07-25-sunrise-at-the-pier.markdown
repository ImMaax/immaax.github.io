I already had this shot in mind when I planned this vacation. Up until today, I
always woke up too late in order to actually take it. Today, I woke up at 5am,
no alarm, and went to work.

Had I waited for a few more minutes, I might have caught the giant red sun
rising, but considering how many people were walking onto the pier to enjoy the
sunrise at that point, it probably would not have provided for a better shot
anyway.
