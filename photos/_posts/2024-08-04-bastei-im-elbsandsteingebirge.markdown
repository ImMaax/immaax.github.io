It was terribly crowded and people literally yelled at me and applauded once I
tore down my tripod and left. I think it's sad that tourists behave like this
towards others just because they take a minute or two instead of ten seconds or
so for their photo. Where I travel, I like to appreciate what I see, not just
quickly skim over and leave.

The weather wasn't exactly as one would have liked, given the tremendous
scenery. I'll need to go back there some day, preferably in the early morning
hours with a clearer sky.
