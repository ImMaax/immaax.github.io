Shot last summer when the
[Wasserspiele](https://www.kassel.de/buerger/kunst_und_kultur/parks_und_gaerten/wilhelmshoehe/wasserspiele.php)
were taking place.

I should have used a stronger GND filter in hindsight, considering how
overexposed the clouds are.

There were a lot of travellers there that day. Getting shots with not too many
people in them was quite the challenge. If you look closely, you will spot some
people even in this photo.
