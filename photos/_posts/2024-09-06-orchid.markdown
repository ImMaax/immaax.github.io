It's been quite a while since I last developed my own film. I used to do it on
and off some time ago, but eventually stopped it entirely because I put my large
format camera to the side in favor of my
[Mamiya SLR](https://goetz.pro/articles/2024/03/19/the-mamiya-rb67-camera-system).
Eventually, I sold my large format camera as I didn't think it fit my workflow
so well. Recently though, my interest in large format was sparked again and I
decided to pursue a field camera, this time around: a brand new Intrepid 4x5!

And that meant getting back into home developing too as having sheet film
developed by my lab is pretty expensive. Besides, I somewhat felt that I was
giving control over my photos away by having someone else do the developing for
me, but maybe I'm just talking myself into it a little more.

Either way, for being the first proper sheet I developed in ages, I think this
one turned out quite nicely! The difficult (available) lighting situation makes
it quite interesting.
